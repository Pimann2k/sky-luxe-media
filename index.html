Questions

Common questions

How much does property and aerial media cost in Brisbane?
Our packages start from $250 for the Property Starter Pack (aerial photos, ground-level photos and a reel). The Sky Story Reel starts from $350 and the Builder Progress Pack starts from $450. Custom quotes are always available — pricing depends on scope and travel distance.
How quickly are photos and videos delivered?
Edited galleries are delivered online within 24–48 hours of the shoot — fast enough to keep your listing or campaign momentum.
Are you CASA-accredited?
Yes. SkyLuxe Media operates as a CASA-accredited drone operator and completes airspace, weather and site safety checks before every flight.
What are AI-assisted concept visuals?
Some of our portfolio and concept work uses AI-assisted tools to create cinematic intros, branding visuals and storytelling support. These are clearly labelled and are never used to misrepresent actual property features or real locations.
Do you do ground-level photos as well as drone?
Yes. Most of our packages include both aerial drone shots and ground-level exterior and detail photos — giving you the complete set of images needed for listings, socials and marketing.
What areas do you service?
Greater Brisbane and surrounding South East Queensland. Travel further afield can be arranged — just mention the location in your enquiry.
